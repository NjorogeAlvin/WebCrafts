KABARAK UNIVERSITY

ABOUT

History

Kabarak University is a private Christian university located in Nakuru, Kenya. It was established in 2002 and is known for its commitment to academic excellence and holistic education.The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various fields including business, education, health sciences, law, and social sciences.

Location

The University is located in Nakuru County, about 20 km from Nakuru town and 12 km off the Nakuru-Eldama Ravine road.Main campus is on a 600-acre site near Nakuru, with a modern Nakuru City Campus for convenience, offering a peaceful learning environment.

Academic Programs

Schools

Academic programs in Kabarak are spread across the following seven schools:

  1. School of Business and Economics
  2. School of Education
  3. School of Health Sciences
  4. School of Law
  5. School of Science and Technology
  6. School of Social Sciences
  7. School of Theology and Religious Studies
